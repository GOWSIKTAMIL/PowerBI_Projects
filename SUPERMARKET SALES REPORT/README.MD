# SUPERMARKET SALES REPORT
# Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze supermarket sales performance across product categories, customer segments, payment types, and city branches. This analysis helps identify top-selling product lines, customer purchase behavior, revenue-driving categories, and overall business performance to support better decision-making.

# Project Highlights

This project focuses on key business areas including:

Total gross income

Quantity sold across all product lines

Highest-performance categories

Customer type distribution

Payment method behavior

Branch-wise sales comparison

Rating trends across product categories

These insights help the supermarket understand which categories perform well, how customers behave, and where improvements can enhance sales and customer satisfaction.

# Dashboard Visualization


The dashboard presents a complete overview of supermarket sales using KPIs, tables, bar charts, line charts, and donut charts for interactive analysis.

This section compares customer satisfaction across product lines.

Quantity sold

8. Branch-wise Gross Income

9. Total Sales by City

A pie chart displays sales contribution from:

Naypyitaw

Yangon

# Conclusion

This supermarket sales analysis provides a detailed understanding of business performance across multiple dimensions. Based on the insights, the supermarket can:

Focus on high-performing product lines like Electronic Accessories and increase stock or promotions.

Improve categories with lower ratings by enhancing quality or customer experience.

Analyze peak shopping times to optimize staffing and inventory.

Use branch-wise income data to design branch-specific strategies.

Target promotions based on preferred payment types and customer segments.

Overall, the dashboard helps the management make data-driven decisions to boost sales, improve customer satisfaction, and optimize operations.
